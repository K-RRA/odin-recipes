Lasagna

This is how you make a delicious Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavour
  • Tomato Products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add a subtle sweetness and enhance the flavour of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavoured with fresh parsely, dried basil leaves ,salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or home-made Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta cheese make this Lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the Ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the Lasagna when cutting into it

Cooking process

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Cover the foil and bake.
  6. Let the Lasagna rest before serving.
